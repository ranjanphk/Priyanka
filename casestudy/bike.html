FNF Mobile — Case Study

On-demand bike servicing — pickup, repair, pay & track from your phone

Overview

FNF (Fast & Friendly) is a mobile app that lets motorbike owners schedule full servicing from home. A verified rider picks up the bike, takes it to a partner workshop, completes servicing, and returns it — customers can pay online and view invoices inside the app.

Problem Statement

Finding trustworthy bike servicing is time-consuming. Customers must take time off to drop the bike, wait at the workshop, or trust informal pickup services without clear pricing or digital invoices.

Goals

  • Let customers schedule pickup from their home or workplace
  • Provide transparent pricing and digital invoices
  • Allow secure online payments and status tracking
  • Minimize downtime — fast turnaround and notifications

User Flow

Customer Flow

Sign up → Select bike & service package → Schedule pickup → Track pickup → Approve estimate → Pay online → Track return → Receive invoice

Operator / Workshop Flow

Receive pickup request → Accept & dispatch rider → Update work items → Upload invoice & photos → Complete job → Mark returned

Key Features

  • Home Pickup Scheduling: Choose date/time and pickup address.
  • Service Packages: Basic service, Full service, Customized repairs with estimated costs.
  • Live Tracking: Track the rider picking up and returning the bike.
  • Online Payments: Pay securely via UPI, cards, or wallet.
  • Digital Invoices & Photos: Workshops upload itemized invoices and before/after photos.
  • Ratings & Support: Rate the service and open support tickets.
